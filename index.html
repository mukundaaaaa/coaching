About Us

Learning rooted in tradition,
powered by technology

PRAJNANAM E-Pathsala was founded with a single mission — to make quality education accessible to every student in the region. Our name, drawn from Sanskrit meaning "wisdom" or "knowledge," reflects our commitment to deep learning.

We offer expert-led offline classes for Class 10 and 12 (Science & Arts), while making the entire admission, payment, and progress tracking experience seamlessly digital.
