Section 01

Who We Are

PRAJNANAM E-Pathsala ("PRAJNANAM," "we," "us," or "our") is an educational coaching institute offering offline classes for students of Class 10 and Class 12 (Science and Arts streams), with online admission, fee payment, and progress-tracking facilities.

Our registered email for legal and privacy matters is infoprajnanam@gmail.com.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information about you when you visit our website, create an account, or enroll in our courses.

Section 02

Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Identity data: Full name, date of birth, gender, photograph (if submitted)
  • Contact data: Email address, mobile number, postal/residential address
  • Academic data: Current class, school/board, subjects chosen
  • Account credentials: Username and hashed password
  • Parent/guardian data: Name and contact number (for students under 18)
  • Payment data: Transaction reference numbers, payment method type, amount, and status — we do not store raw card numbers or CVVs
  • Communication data: Messages or queries you send us via email or the website

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, links clicked, time spent, referral URLs
  • Device data: Browser type and version, operating system, screen resolution
  • Log data: IP address, access timestamps, server log entries
  • Cookie data: Session tokens, preference cookies (see Section 5)
⚠️
We do not collect sensitive personal information such as Aadhaar numbers, PAN, caste, religion, or biometric data unless specifically required by law and with your explicit consent.

Section 04

Payment Data & Gateway Security

🔐
Your payment details are processed by a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We do not store, transmit, or process raw card numbers, CVV codes, or full bank account details on our servers.

When you make a payment on our platform, the transaction is processed by a third-party payment gateway (such as Razorpay, PayU, or Cashfree — the specific provider will be clearly identified at checkout). These providers are certified under PCI-DSS and compliant with RBI guidelines for payment aggregators in India.

What we store after payment is completed:

  • Payment transaction ID (for reference and reconciliation)
  • Amount paid, date, and course enrolled
  • Payment status (success / failure / pending)
  • Payment method type in general terms (e.g., "UPI" or "Debit Card" — not the card number)

For payment disputes or failed transactions, please contact us at infoprajnanam@gmail.com within 7 days of the transaction.

Section 06

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information only as necessary with:

  • Payment Gateway Providers: To process your fee payments securely (e.g., Razorpay / PayU / Cashfree)
  • SMS & Email Service Providers: To send OTPs, enrollment confirmations, and fee receipts
  • Cloud Hosting Providers: Our website and database are hosted on secure cloud servers
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, court order, or government authority under applicable Indian law
  • Professional Advisors: Lawyers or accountants, bound by confidentiality obligations

All third-party service providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it solely for the purposes we specify.

Section 07

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain your active student account and course records
  • Comply with applicable tax and financial record-keeping laws (typically 7 years for payment records under Indian law)
  • Resolve disputes or enforce our agreements

Once data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it. You may request deletion of your account data at any time (see Section 8), subject to our legal retention obligations.

Section 08

Your Rights

As a user of PRAJNANAM E-Pathsala, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for optional data processing (e.g., promotional emails) at any time
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: File a complaint with us or with the relevant authority under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at infoprajnanam@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days.

Section 09

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for students of Class 10 and Class 12, many of whom may be under 18 years of age. We take the privacy of minors seriously:

  • For students under 18, parental or guardian consent is obtained at the time of enrollment
  • A parent/guardian name and contact number are collected and linked to the student's account
  • We do not display advertising to minors on our platform
  • We do not knowingly share data of minors with third parties for marketing

Parents or guardians may contact us at any time to review, correct, or request deletion of their child's data.

Section 10

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Passwords stored as salted hashes — never in plain text
  • Access to personal data restricted to authorized staff only
  • Regular security reviews of our systems
  • Payment processing handled entirely by PCI-DSS certified providers

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

Section 11

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify registered users via email if the changes are material
  • Post a prominent notice on our website

Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
